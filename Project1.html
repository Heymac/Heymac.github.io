Sprout

Indoor Hydroponics Appliance

5 Month / Individual Project

Objective

To design an automatic hydroponics system that waters, feeds, and regulates the environment of the owner’s plants.

Research

I first cataloged existing products in order to get a sense of the market and what features were currently out there.
I conducted a survey of 110 owners of hydroponics appliances. I asked what plants they grow, why they bought their appliance, and what they would look for in a new model.
From these insights I was able to create five specific criteria:
1. The system MUST be able to grow varying sizes of plants, from Microgreens to Tomatoes (1/4’’ think sheet, 4’’ tall — ~400in^3, 4’ tall)
2. The system SHOULD be size adjustable or scalable (modular)
3. The system SHOULD allow "aftermarket" upgrades
4. The system OUGHT to be easily disassemblable for cleaning
5. The system OUGHT to have the capability to be automated
