Finishing Touches

Commercial Website

2 Month / Team Project

Objective

To design a website for a local furniture store that allows clients to order custom items

Background

This business started as a hobby for a home contractor who produces wooden furniture made to custom sizes. He was starting to receive more orders by word of mouth, and wanted to expand his online presence to organize orders and show a full listing of available furniture.
